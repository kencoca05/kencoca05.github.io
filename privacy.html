Privacy Policy

This application uses OAuth-based permissions to access user information from TikTok and Google Drive.

Data We Collect

  • OAuth tokens for TikTok and Google Drive, stored securely and used only to perform the actions you authorize.
  • Basic profile information provided by TikTok APIs.
  • No videos or personal files are stored permanently on our servers.

How We Use Your Data

  • To upload videos to TikTok on your behalf.
  • To list and access video files in your Google Drive, only after permission is granted.
  • To operate and improve the app functionality.

Data Sharing

We do not sell or share your personal data with third parties. Data is only transferred to TikTok or Google as required by their APIs.

Sandbox Notice

This Privacy Policy applies to a sandbox testing environment and does not represent a production-level service.
